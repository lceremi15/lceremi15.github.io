I am a student so money is a scarce resource.

As a result I like to do stuff outdoors and go out and spend money only in interesting and unique places.

Below you will find out what my favorite spot
in Klaipeda's nature is!

Olando Kepure

Olando Kepure holds a special place in my heart. Apart from it being the most beautiful place in the region of Klaipeda, it is also a place where I come to relax and be with myself and my thoughts. The calming sound of waves crashing and the sound of trees swaying in the wind really calms me down and lets me tune out everything else.
