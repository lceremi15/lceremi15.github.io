Place in City

I am a student so money is a scarce resource.

As a result I like to do stuff outdoors and go out and spend money only in interesting and unique places.

Below you will find out what my favorite spot
in Klaipeda's city is!

Circle K near LCC

My favorite place in the city of Klaipeda is the gas station Circle K. It actually is more than a gas station. To me it is a place to chat and to taste the best burger I have found in Klaipeda.

One of my buddies from school works there and he always has some interesting things to say whenever he visits and he always makes me the best burger. The burger that I speak of is called "Wrap burger". It is a new item on their food menu and frankly the most popular. Currently they have a promotion where with any hot drink you can take the burger for a total of 3.00 euros!

The gas station itself is run by amazing staff who always greet you with a smile and are willing to chat. I have become a frequent costumer and they now greet me by name. I recommend everyone to visit this place and taste the burger that I have grown so fond of.
